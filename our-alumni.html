Who We Are

The Alumni Association of Chandigarh College of Engineering and Technology (AACCET), is a sub-faction of Training and Placement Cell (TPC) of CCET that offers a platform to unite together the college graduates amongst each other as well as establish a professional relation of college students with their Alumni.

AACCET acts as an active body maintained by Alumni Executives of Training and Placements Cell along with Executive Alumni members of AACCET to provide a platform where students can interact, seek professional guidance and learn about various carrier opportunities. AACCET interact with college Alumni and work together to bring carrier opportunities to students’ doorsteps in the form of campus placements, industrial trainings and internships.

AACCET also plays vital role in organizing various interactive workshops and seminars to bring information and knowledge of corporate world from Alumni’s experience to the students in their respective fields of expertise.
